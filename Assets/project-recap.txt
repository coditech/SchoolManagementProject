This is a website for schools.

It has 4 main users: parents, students, teachers and the school admin.

The parents use this website to see their sons and daughters grades, or to contact specific teachers or the schools administration, or to view school news.

Students use this website to view their grades or to view school news.

Teachers use this website to contact a specific student's parents or to view school news.

The school admin handles making accounts for parents, students and teachers, and linking students to their parents.
